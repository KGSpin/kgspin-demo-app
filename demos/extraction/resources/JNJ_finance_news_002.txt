Title: J&J Completes Shockwave Medical Integration, Eyes Further MedTech Acquisitions
Source: Reuters
Published: Mon, 03 Feb 2026 09:15:00 +0000

Johnson & Johnson announced the completion of its $13.1 billion acquisition of Shockwave Medical, a move that significantly expands its cardiovascular intervention portfolio. The integration, led by MedTech segment leadership, adds intravascular lithotripsy technology to J&J's existing Abiomed heart pump platform.

CEO Joaquin Duato told analysts that the combined cardiovascular portfolio positions Johnson & Johnson as a market leader in interventional cardiology. "With Abiomed and Shockwave together, we offer the most comprehensive suite of technologies for treating complex cardiovascular disease," Duato stated.

The acquisition follows J&J's strategy of building scale in high-growth MedTech categories. The company competes with Abbott Laboratories and Medtronic in the medical devices space. Industry analysts expect further bolt-on acquisitions in surgical robotics and digital health.

CFO Joseph Wolk confirmed that the deal is expected to be accretive to earnings by 2027, with approximately $300 million in annual cost synergies. The FDA has cleared several new indications for Shockwave's C2+ coronary system since the acquisition closed.
